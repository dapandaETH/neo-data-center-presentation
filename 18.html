Technical Risks

Infrastructure & Technology Challenges

⚠️

GPU Compatibility

Medium

Challenge: GPU driver and CUDA version conflicts across different hardware generations (RTX 3090, 4090, A6000, A100)

Mitigation Strategy

  • Thorough testing in staging environment
  • Standardize on NVIDIA GPU Operator
  • Version-controlled driver images
  • Pre-deployment compatibility matrix
⚡

Network Bottlenecks

Medium

Challenge: Inter-node communication latency and GPU-to-GPU data transfer limitations

Mitigation Strategy

  • 25GbE networking for high bandwidth
  • NVLink optimization for A100/H100
  • Network topology optimization
  • Latency monitoring and alerting

Operational Risks

Process & Team Challenges

🔥

Single Points of Failure

High

Challenge: Critical infrastructure components without redundancy could cause service-wide outages

Mitigation Strategy

  • Multi-node redundancy (control plane: 3 nodes)
  • PodDisruptionBudgets for HA
  • Cross-zone deployment where possible
  • Disaster recovery procedures
💸

Cost Overruns

Medium

Challenge: Unexpected infrastructure costs, GPU compute expenses, and operational overhead

Mitigation Strategy

  • GPU sharing for maximum utilization
  • Dynamic scaling based on demand
  • Spot instances for dev/staging
  • Cost tracking dashboards
  • Budget monitoring and alerts
📊 Continuous risk assessment via Prometheus monitoring and weekly retrospectives
